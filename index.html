📘 About Bharat Oil Carrier

Bharat Oil Carrier is India’s leading logistics partner for bulk bitumen and liquid chemical transport. Based in Mathura near Refinery Gate No. 9, we offer nationwide delivery with our modern, GPS-enabled fleet ranging from 19MT to 33MT capacity.

Our operations span across Mathura, Panipat, Vadodara, Haldia, Chennai, Mumbai and more – catering to both government and private infrastructure projects. We follow strict safety protocols, on-time delivery guarantees, and transparent billing for complete peace of mind.

With over a decade of experience in bitumen transport logistics, we understand the unique challenges of road construction supply chains and deliver custom transport solutions across all terrains – highways, hills, and interiors.
